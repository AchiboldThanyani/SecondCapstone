Our Story

At The Modern Restaurant, we believe in providing an exceptional dining experience with a blend of contemporary and classic cuisines. Our dedicated team of chefs use the freshest ingredients to create mouth-watering dishes that cater to all tastes. Located in the heart of the city, The Modern Restaurant offers a sophisticated and relaxing ambiance perfect for any gathering. Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey and discover why we are a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Our chefs are passionate about using only the freshest ingredients to create dishes that are both delicious and visually appealing. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner for two or a special occasion celebration, The Modern Restaurant is the perfect choice. We offer a variety of dining options, including private rooms and outdoor seating areas, to ensure that every guest has a comfortable and enjoyable experience. Our menu features a range of international dishes, from classic comfort food to exotic cuisine from around the world. From business lunches to family gatherings, we have a menu and atmosphere that will suit your needs. Our team of experienced waiters and bartenders are dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every guest has an unforgettable experience. At The Modern Restaurant, we are committed to sustainability and strive to reduce our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and waste management practices. We are proud to be a part of the local community and are committed to supporting local suppliers and charities. Whether you're a foodie or just looking for a great meal, we invite you to come and experience The Modern Restaurant for yourself. Join us for lunch or dinner today and discover why we are a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
